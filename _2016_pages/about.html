Our Mission

Pravesha is a national Inter-College Tech Symposium that takes place every year with the help of our hardworking computer science students who dedicate themselves to bring out a fun filled as well as a challenging symposium with various events to participate on. Now is the time where the hidden talents could show themselves to the crowd of what their skill sets are by participating one of the many events that fits their capabilities. Be it technical or Non-technical there is oppurtinity for everyone in everything. Our hearty welcome to one and all joining us on the day of event.
